# Gin and Lemon Tonic
## Ingredients

- 100g ice
- 30g  gin
- 37g  lemon juice
- 30g  [Simple Syrup](SimpleSyrup.md)
- 100g club soda
## Equipment
Dealer's choice of mixing equipment
## Instructions
Shake it or stir it. Up to you. Just make sure you add the soda *after* shaking if you go that route
